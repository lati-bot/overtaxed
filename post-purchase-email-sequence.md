## Email 2: Day 21 Follow-up - "Your hearing is coming up"

**Subject Line:** 📅 Your property tax hearing is coming up — review your script

**Body:**
```
Hi [Customer Name],

Your property tax protest hearing should be scheduled soon (if it hasn't been already). Time to prepare for success!

**Your hearing prep checklist:**

✓ **Review your script** (included in your original packet)
✓ **Practice key talking points:**
   - Market value evidence 
   - Comparable property data
   - Property condition factors

✓ **Bring these materials:**
   - Photo copies of all documents (originals for backup)
   - Property photos if applicable
   - Your completed script/talking points

**What to expect:**
- **Informal review first:** Most counties offer this before a formal hearing
- **15-30 minute meeting** with an appraiser or review officer
- **Stay factual:** Stick to market data, avoid emotional arguments
- **Be reasonable:** A 10-20% reduction is often achievable

**Common hearing mistakes to avoid:**
❌ Arguing about tax rates (focus on appraised value only)
❌ Using Zillow estimates (use actual sales data)
❌ Being confrontational (stay professional and factual)
❌ Accepting first offers too quickly (counter-negotiate)

**Need a refresher?**
- Download your materials again: [Customer Portal Link]  
- Hearing tips and scripts: [Resources Link]
- Questions? Email: support@getovertaxed.com

**Pro tip:** If your informal review doesn't go well, you can still request a formal Appraisal Review Board hearing. Don't give up after the first meeting!

Rooting for your success,

The GetOvertaxed Team

P.S. Our customers average $1,247 in annual tax savings. You've got this! 💪
```
